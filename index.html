Indus Public School


Indus Public School is spread over a vast lush green campus on National Highway-10. 30 minutes drive from Delhi Border. The School is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi up to XII level providing education in Medical, Non-Medical, Commerce Streams. Every initiative, effort at the upcoming institute is single-mindedly focused on these three basic values.

A Societal Mission

Our mission is to contribute to the world by educating students for lives of leadership and contribution with integrity; advancing fundamental knowledge and cultivating creativity; leading in pioneering research for effective clinical therapies; and accelerating solutions and amplifying their impact.

Our Vision

Indus Public School has been established with the vision to initiate a ceaseless process of searching qualitative improvement in response to changes taking place in the society. The school is developing a delivery system to make the vision of the founder a vibrant reality. National curriculum Framework of School Education 2000 A.D. talks about multiple intelligence as opposed to the traditional concept of intelligence. Multiple intelligence views every individual as a unique person and the uniqueness influences the various ways in which learning takes place, an idea like this, which does not hold intelligence as a unitary concept, but a multiple attribute promotes personalization of education by connecting the learner’s total life to the learning in the classroom. Indus Public School shall endeavor to put this concept in practice. This will ensure success to many and hence better level of self-esteem amongst students.
